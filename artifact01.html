Black Mango is an online clothing store which sells staple wardrobe pieces and athleisure clothing. This project is to design an ui/ux interface for their app using figma so that clients have a better understanding with the visual presentation.

Functionalities

Since Black Mango is an online store, functionalities on the app become crucial as majority of the users will place orders on the app. According to statictics, around 60% of internet users visit sites through mobile devices. Studies said that the percentage of mobile users would keep increasing due to the portability aspect. To maximize our users base, optimizing the app is the prioritized task on the list.

Wireframes

Rose and I built the wireframes together before we got the color scheme and logo from the design team. After several research and analysis from competitiors such as Aliexpress and Temu, we found out that an organized categories enhance users experience. Meanwhile, the process from checking the product page to placing orders was one of the major tasks that we was focusing on. To have a better communication with the design team, we put some indications on the wireframes just so team members understand what kind of contents are being put on that specific page.
