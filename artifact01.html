Overview

Black Mango is an online clothing store that sells staple wardrobe pieces and athleisure clothing. This project is to design a UI/UX interface for their app using Figma so that clients have a better understanding through visual presentation.

Functionalities

Since Black Mango is an online store, functionalities on the app become crucial, as the majority of users will place orders on the app. According to statistics, around 60% of internet users visit sites through mobile devices. Studies suggest that the percentage of mobile users will keep increasing due to the portability aspect. To maximize our user base, optimizing the app is the prioritized task on the list.

Wireframes

Rose and I built the wireframes together before we got the color scheme and logo from the design team. After several research and analyses from competitors such as Aliexpress and Temu, we found out that organized categories enhance users' experience. Meanwhile, the process from checking the product page to placing orders was one of the major tasks that we were focusing on. To have better communication with the design team, we put some indications on the wireframes just so team members understand what kind of contents are being put on that specific page.
