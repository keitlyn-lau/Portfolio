faai3 Cafe Website

The Faai3 Café website was a fictitious business run by a Hongkongese in Vancouver. The fictitious owner requested a responsive website that responds quickly when end-users visit it. This required the skill to optimize picture sizes, build effective codes, and undergo a UI/UX development phase.
