faai3 Cafe Website

The faai3 Café website was a fictitious business which was run by a Hongkongese in Vancouver. The fictitious owner requested to have a responsive website which responds quickly when end users visit it. This required the skill to optimize pictures sizes, to build effective codes, and a ui/ux development phrase.
